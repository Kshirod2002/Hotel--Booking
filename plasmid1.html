Room Types :

Single Occupancy Room

A room assigned to one person. May have one or more beds. The room size or area of single Rooms are generally between 37 m' to 45 m'.

Family Room

A parlour or living room connected with to one or more bedroom.(A room with one or more bedroom and a separate living space.) The room size or area of Suite rooms are generally between 70 m' to 100 m'.

Duplex Room

The most expensive room provided by a hotel. Usually, only one suplex suite is available in one single hotel property.Duplex suite always has one or more bedrooms and a living space with a strong emphasis on grand in room decoration, high quality amenities and supplies and tailor made services.
